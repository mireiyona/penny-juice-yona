ABOUT PENNY JUICE

At Penny Juice, we believe that healthy living can be fun and delicious! We’re all about making fresh, tasty, and nutritious juices that kids and families love. Our mission is to bring smiles to every sip while helping kids grow stronger, healthier, and more energized.

HISTORY OF PENNY JUICE

It all began on a sunny afternoon in the small town of Orchardville. Emma, a mom of two, was on a mission to get her kids to enjoy healthy food without any fuss. She knew how hard it was to get little ones to drink anything that wasn’t filled with sugar, so she decided to create something that would make them want to sip and smile. Armed with a blender, a basket of fresh fruits, and a pinch of creativity, Emma started experimenting in her kitchen.
